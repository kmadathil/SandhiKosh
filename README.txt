SandhiKosh

Sandhi Kosh is the first Sanskrit Sandhi Benchmark created to evaluate the Sanskrit Sandhi Tools. The corpus is provided below free of cost for research purposes only. If you use this corups in your research, please cite the following paper:

Bhardwaj, S., Gantayat, N., Chaturvedi, N., Garg, R., & Agarwal, S. (2018, May). Sandhikosh: A benchmark corpus for evaluating sanskrit sandhi tools. In Proceedings of the Eleventh International Conference on Language Resources and Evaluation (LREC 2018).
